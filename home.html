About Parul University

Parul University is a leading multidisciplinary private university located in Vadodara, Gujarat. Spread across a vast and vibrant campus, it offers a diverse range of academic programs in engineering, medicine, pharmacy, management, arts, sciences, and technology. The university is home to thousands of students from across India and abroad and is known for its innovation-driven education, modern infrastructure, and strong industry collaboration.

The expansive campus includes academic blocks, administrative buildings, hostels, laboratories, libraries, sports complexes, and recreational spaces, making navigation a challenge for new students and visitors.

What This Platform Provides

This AR/VR Campus Navigation platform is designed to provide an immersive 360° virtual exploration of Parul University. It allows students, parents, and visitors to explore different campus locations digitally before physically visiting the university.

Through interactive 360° panoramic scenes and smart navigation hotspots, users can move between buildings, understand campus layout, and access important location information easily.

The system enhances accessibility, reduces confusion, and introduces a futuristic digital navigation experience.
